Crystal Collector Instructions

You will be give a random number at the start of the game.

Each of the 4 crystals has a different (hidden) point value. When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If your total score goes over the random number, you lose!

Each time the game restarts, the values of the crystals change.
